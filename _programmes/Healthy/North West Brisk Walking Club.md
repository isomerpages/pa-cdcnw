Officially launched in 2002, the **North West Brisk Walking Club** has grown to become the largest brisk walking club in Singapore.

Members of the North West Brisk Walking Club participate in weekly walks around their residential estates and enjoy occasional brisk walking trips around different scenic locations across Singapore. Novelty brisk walk events have also been organised for the members, such as the North West 15 km Brisk Walk Challenge and North West Walk N Shop. In addition to promoting health, brisk walking is also a great platform for the members to bond and build friendships.

Residents interested in joining the North West Brisk Walking Club may visit their nearest Residents’ Committee (RC).
