<p><strong>Street Dance @ North West</strong> provides a platform for youth
to showcase their talent and sharpen their dance skills with like-minded
individuals. The event features various dance styles, including hip-hop,
locking and popping through an innovative blend of digital and physical
formats that cater to different participation preferences.</p>
<p>The highlight of the event is the physical dance battle, taking place
at Bukit Panjang Plaza on 28 March 2026, from 12pm to 8pm. Interested participants
may sign up <a href="https://go.gov.sg/nwdance-reg" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">here</a>.</p>
<p>An online dance challenge is also available for those looking to showcase
their moves to trending songs from the comfort of their own space. Entries
for the online challenge can be submitted <a href="https://go.gov.sg/dancechallenge-submit" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">here</a>.</p>
<p>For enquiries, please contact North West CDC at <a href="mailto:northwest_cdc@pa.gov.sg" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank"><u>northwest_cdc@pa.gov.sg</u></a>.</p>
