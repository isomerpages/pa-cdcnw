<p>Started in 2025, <strong>Dance @ North West</strong> provides a platform
for youth to showcase their talent and sharpen their dance skills with
like-minded individuals. The event features various dance styles, including
hip-hop, locking and popping through an innovative blend of digital and
physical formats that cater to different participation preferences.</p>
