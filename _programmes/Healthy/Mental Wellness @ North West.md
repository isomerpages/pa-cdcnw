<p><strong>Mental Wellness @ North West</strong>&nbsp;is part of the CDC's
Healthy Living @ North West programmes in promoting holistic wellness.
Launched in 2021, the initiative aims to raise awareness of mental wellness
in the community.</p>
<p>&nbsp;The programme brings together healthcare experts, community and
corporate partners, and fellow residents to build up a mental wellness
support network through various programmes In collaboration with PsyConnect-a
psychology student interest group comprising undergraduates and alumni
from SUSS, the <strong>Mental Wellness Toolkit </strong>was developed. This
toolkit serves as a resource guide for caregivers and residents to better
understand mental health conditions and identify available resources within
the North West District.</p>
<p>&nbsp;Download the Mental Wellness Toolkit available in four languages
-&nbsp;<a href="https://go.gov.sg/mwtoolkit" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">English</a>,&nbsp;
<a href="https://go.gov.sg/mwtoolkitmandarin" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">Mandarin</a>,&nbsp;<a href="https://go.gov.sg/mwtoolkitmalay" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">Malay</a>&nbsp;and&nbsp;<a href="https://go.gov.sg/mwtoolkittamil" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">Tamil</a>.</p>
<p>&nbsp;To encourage ground-up initiatives that promote mental well-being
in the community, community organisations may apply for the <strong>North West WeCare Fund </strong>to
support event expenses.</p>
<p>Launched in 2024, Mental Wellness Day @ North West is a flagship event
designed to raise awareness of the importance of mental health in the community.
North West CDC partnered with healthcare experts, community and corporate
partners to conduct workshops and set up booths to educate participants
about mental health, reduce stigma, and encourage open discussions.</p>
