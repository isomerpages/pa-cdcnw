<p>The rising cost of living has placed increasing pressure on households
across Singapore in recent years. Everyday essentials, including food,
have become a growing concern for many families. Ready Meals @ North West
is a practical response to this reality, ensuring that residents across
the North West have access to <strong>affordable</strong> and <strong>convenient</strong> meals
whenever they need them.</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/Ready_Meals_Logo__2_.png">
</div>
<p><strong>About the Programme</strong>
</p>
<p>Ready Meals @ North West is an initiative by North West CDC that brings
affordable and convenient meals directly to residents across the North
West. With meals priced below $3.00, the programme aims to ease cost-of-living
pressures and improve food accessibility for all residents, regardless
of background or circumstance.</p>
<p>The vending machines operate 24/7, extending convenience for all residents
to purchase affordable ready-to-eat meals. As part of Phase 1 of the launch
of these vending machines, they will be deployed across the 14 North West
Divisions within proximity of public rental blocks, bringing this cost-of-living
initiative directly to the low-income residents in our community.</p>
<p>Identified low-income households will also be able to redeem meals for
free through a dedicated credit system, and thus, serve as a value addition
to the CDC’s ecosystem of support, complementing current existing schemes
such as Project Sama Sama @ North West, North West Food Aid Fund and the
North West Service Weeks.</p>
<p>By the fourth quarter of 2026, the programme will expand to up to 100
vending machines across all 20 Divisions in the North West, bringing affordable
food options within reach of every resident in the community.</p>
<p>Ready Meals @ North West is part of North West CDC's broader effort to
support residents through the challenges of daily life. By making affordable
meals accessible around the clock, the programme aims to provide practical,
tangible relief to those who need it, in line with our commitment to building
a Caring and Healthy North West.</p>
