### **Sustainability in Every Bite!**

Feast on **bite-sized food sustainability tips**, explore our **weekend Farmers’ Market** with fresh local produce and delicious food stalls, attend **green workshops and activities**, and participate in **celebrity cooking demos** turning ugly food into delicious meals! 

Up for some competition? You can join our **cooking contests** to level up and compete with other inspiring chefs to be! 

Best of all, the three-day festival is **FREE** for all! So join us on this immersive food journey, complete a quiz and stand a chance to win attractive prizes! **Sign up for our workshops to experience hands-on green activities and learn tips to start your green practice at home!**
