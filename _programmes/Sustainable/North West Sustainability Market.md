<p><strong>Programme Highlights</strong>&nbsp;</p>
<p>The North West Sustainability Market is here! Join us to learn about healthy,
eco-friendly eating habits and how to reduce food waste while shopping
for affordable locally sourced produce at the farmers’ market!&nbsp;&nbsp;</p>
<p>There will be cooking demonstrations featuring healthy, delicious recipes,
and plant-based diets, alongside hands-on workshops on growing your own
vegetables at home, and tips on how to extend the shelf life of fruits
and vegetables.&nbsp;</p>
<p>Don’t miss our food rescue distribution, where “ugly” fruits and vegetables
will be given out to the community!&nbsp;</p>
<p>There are activities for everyone, so bring your family along! Admission
to the North West Sustainability Market is free.&nbsp;</p>
<p></p>
