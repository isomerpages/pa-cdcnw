<p>The highly anticipated SkillsFuture Festival @ North West has returned,
offering an expanded range of opportunities to empower individuals’ skills
upgrading and reskilling, to make lifelong learning our way of life. Look
out for an array of curated workshops, activities, and job fairs to support
and guide you about the skills and trends in three emerging economies –
Care, Digital, Green.</p>

<p><strong><u>Event Highlights</u></strong>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p><strong>Explore Zone:</strong> Discover courses to upskill and find job
opportunities. Engage with various exhibitors to learn how you can advance
your career.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Experiential Zone: </strong>Participate in hands-on activities
(e.g. Drone Flying, AI tools)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Guidance Zone:</strong> Looking for a career switch or advice on
your lifelong learning journey? The SkillsFuture Advice @ North West team
and career coaches will be on-site to understand your career interests
and goals, and receive personalised advice on training, tools, and resources
to support your career transition or progression.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Exclusive Talks: </strong>Attend our talks on in-demand topics
to stay ahead of industry trends. Register <a href="https://go.gov.sg/sfanorthwest" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">here</a>!</p>
</li>
</ul>
