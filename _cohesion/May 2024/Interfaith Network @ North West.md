<p>&lt;p style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 26px;"&gt;Inclusivity, harmony
and tolerance are values we hold dear in our multicultural and multireligious
society. Through Interfaith Network @ North West, faith institutions in
the North West have opened their doors to people from other faiths, hosting
site tours and interfaith dialogues to build bridges and foster trust.
Let’s revisit these journeys together. &lt;/p&gt;</p>
