a group of dedicated volunteers turn up without fail to tend to the crops

known. Located opposite Blk 320 Yishun Central, the community farm was
built using recycled materials and has become a labour of love for the
residents involved.&lt;br/&gt;&lt;br/&gt;</p>
<p>Discover how the journey began, and how the community farm has rekindled
the kampung spirit and brought sustainability to life!</p>
