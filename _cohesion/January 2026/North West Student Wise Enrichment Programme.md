<div style="padding: 10px 0px 0px;">
<p style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 26px;">On 25 October 2025, 228 students from less-privileged backgrounds across 17 North West schools marked a meaningful milestone as they graduated from the North West Student Wise Enrichment Programme (SWEP). It proved one thing: when given the opportunity, students can thrive and shine!
<br><br>
Besides financial literacy, social etiquette, public speaking and goal setting, the programme's new digital workshops, including AI art and Minecraft coding, helped them unlock fresh digital skills.</p>
</div>

<div style="padding: 15px 0px 0px;">
  <p style="font-size: 24px; font-weight: bold; text-align: left; color:#D5262B; margin: 0px 0px;">
    <b style="color: #D5262B;">Digital Bootcamp for Families and Children on Graduation Day </b>
  </p>

  <p style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 26px;">On Graduation Day, families joined in to build virtual homes in a Digital Bootcamp. The students showed their parents how to design their ideal homes for the future using AI tools that they had learnt in the workshops.</p>

  <br>

  <p style="font-size: 24px; font-weight: bold; text-align: left; color:#D5262B; margin: 0px 0px;">
    <b style="color: #D5262B;">Digital Literacy Takes Centre Stage</b>
  </p>

  <img style="width: 100%; margin-top: 0px; padding: 20px 0px;" class="" src="/images/Cohesion/Jan%202026/digital_literacy.png">


  <p style="font-size: 24px; font-weight: bold; text-align: left; color:#D5262B; margin: 0px 0px; padding: 20px 0px 0px;">
    <b style="color: #D5262B;">Exploring Creativity with AI </b>
  </p>

  <img style="width: 100%; margin-top: 0px; padding: 20px 0px;" class="" src="/images/Cohesion/Jan%202026/Image_207.gif">

  <p style="font-size: 20px; font-weight: bold; text-align: left; color:#D5262B; margin: 0px 0px;">
    <b style="color: #D5262B;">Wan Zafran Yafii'<br>  
Marsiling Primary School </b>
  </p>
  
  <p style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 26px;">Before the workshop, I'd only heard about AI in shows and games. Now, I've learnt more about how to use AI. It helps us to do things faster, like finding information or coming up with ideas.</p>
<p style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 26px;">I made posters for Teacher's Day and Children's Day using Canva AI. I added beautiful images, sparkles and nice text to make them look awesome. I can now put my AI art skills to good use to create presentations and posters.</p>
<p style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 26px;">I also enjoyed the Financial Literacy programme. I learnt to manage my money seriously. I even created a mini budget plan to track my spending at the school canteen.</p>

<img style="width: 100%; margin-top: 0px; padding: 20px 0px;" class="" src="/images/Cohesion/Jan%202026/quote_wan.png">

  </p>

  <p style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 26px;">Mr Cham Bao Rong and Mrs Lee Hui Feng from Qihua Primary School share what they noticed most about the students' growth and development.</p>
